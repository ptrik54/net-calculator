# net-calculator
Calculate your net income, just enter your gross income and the taxes as a percentage.

### Installation

A guide that shows you how to get the app working.

```
$ Install the .APK file on your Android device
```

### Usage

Calculate your net income, just enter your gross income and the taxes as a percentage.

```
--- Example ---
$ Gross - 1500
$ Percent - 14
$ Net - 1290
```
